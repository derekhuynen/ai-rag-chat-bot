## About Contoso

Contoso Ltd. is a fictional mid-sized software company that builds productivity
tools for small and medium businesses. Founded in 2015, Contoso operates fully
remote with around 120 employees across product, engineering, design, and
customer success.

## Product lines

- **Contoso Desk** - a shared inbox and helpdesk for support teams.
- **Contoso Insights** - a lightweight analytics dashboard for SMB operators.
- **Contoso Flow** - a no-code automation builder for internal workflows.

## How Contoso uses AI

Contoso has invested in several AI-powered initiatives over the past two years,
ranging from internal proofs of concept to shipped product features:

- An AI customer support assistant inside Contoso Desk (see SmartDesk).
- A demand and inventory forecasting model for retail customers.
- An internal document-generation proof of concept for sales proposals.

Most AI work is built on managed cloud AI services, with a strong preference for
keyless authentication, small and cost-efficient models, and retrieval-augmented
generation over the company's own knowledge base.

## Engineering principles

1. **Cheap by default.** Prefer serverless and scale-to-zero infrastructure.
2. **Keyless everywhere.** Use managed identity instead of stored secrets.
3. **Tested and gated.** Every change runs through automated CI before merge.
4. **Document as you build.** Architecture decisions live next to the code.
